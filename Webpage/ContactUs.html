FROM CHENNAI CENTRAL STATION:

Those who arrive at the central station may take a sub-urban electric train to Ambattur Station and take an Auto to Velammal Engineering College. (Auto Fare: Rs 100/-to Rs 150/-).

FROM KOYEMBEDU MOFUSSIL BUS TERMINUS:

Those who arrive at the koyambedu mofussil bus terminus can take a bus either to Ambattur O.T. or to Puzhal Camp and then take an Auto to Velammal Engineering College.
